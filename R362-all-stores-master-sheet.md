# Payment / payout doctrine

For each platform, you'll typically need ONE of:
- **PayPal** — simplest, available on Gumroad, RB, S6, TP, Spreadshirt, INPRNT, Zazzle (small fee per payout)
- **Bank ACH** — direct deposit, available on most US platforms after W-9
- **Stripe** — Etsy uses Etsy Payments which is basically Stripe-backed
- **Wire/Check** — Zazzle / older platforms

**Recommended payout setup:**
1. Same PayPal email across every platform (`chrisspangler43@gmail.com` works if that's your PayPal)
2. **Don't enter SSN/EIN until you hit ~$200 lifetime sales per platform.** Most platforms only force the W-9 step at year-end or at payout threshold.
3. Use a virtual mailbox address for any "business address required" field once you cross $200 MRR (R350 doctrine, importance 99 — no home address public).
